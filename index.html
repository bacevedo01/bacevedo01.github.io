Hot Sauces


Hot Sauces are made from chili peppers and can be easy and fun to make at home.

A short list of supplies you need

  • Peppers
  • Glass jar or crock
  • Distilled water
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Cheesecloth
Pepper Heat in Scoville
Jalepeno 2500-8000
Serrano 10,000-25,000
Ghost 855,000-1,047,427
